Alma Rose Quartett

Alma Rosé Quartett is a Vienna-based chamber ensemble dedicated to primaraly 20th-century repertoire and to preserving the legacy of composers whose lives and work were affected by persecution and oppression.
I created full brand identity for Alma Rosé Quartett, including logo design, photography, website design, album cover and booklet design, poster design, and Instagram content. All creative direction and design work by me. My final project at the design school, made for my wife's quartett.
